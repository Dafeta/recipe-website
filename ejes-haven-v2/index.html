Healthy Tips

Stay Hydrated

Drink at least 8 glasses (≈ 2 litres) of water daily to improve digestion and energy levels.

Eat More Greens

Add leafy vegetables (spinach, kale, ugu) to your meals for vitamins, fibre and to boost immunity.

Get Enough Rest

Ensure you get 7-9 hours of quality sleep each night to support overall health. Good sleep helps with better metabolism and appetite control.

Mindful Eating

Eat slowly and chew well, it helps digestion and prevents overeating. Pay attention to your hunger and fullness cues to avoid overeating and enjoy your meals more.

Limit Sugar & Salt

Use natural spices/herbs instead of excess salt or sugar to flavour meals.

Stay Active

Engage in regular physical activity to maintain a healthy weight and improve overall well-being.

Portion Control

Be mindful of portion sizes to avoid overeating and maintain a healthy weight.

Include Colorful Foods

Use a variety of colours (red, yellow, green, orange), fruits & veggies, to get diverse nutrients.

Balance Your Plate

Ensure your meals have a good balance of protein, carbohydrates, and healthy fats for optimal nutrition and lasting fullness.

Plan Meals Ahead

Prepare weekly menus or ingredients ahead to avoid impulsive, unhealthy food choices.
